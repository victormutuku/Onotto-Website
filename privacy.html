Privacy Policy for Onotto - Note Taking Application

Effective Date: 8/31/2023

Thank you for using Onotto, a note-taking application developed by VM Develops. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Onotto application. By using Onotto, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect:

  1. User-Generated Content: Onotto allows you to create and store notes, images, and other
    content on your device. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  2. Device Information: We may collect certain information about your device, such as the
    device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers. This information helps us
    improve the application's compatibility and performance.

How We Use Your Information:

  1. Improvement of Services: We use device information to analyze trends, troubleshoot
    issues, and enhance the overall performance and user experience of the application.
  2. No Transmission of Data: Onotto operates on a local device basis. We do not collect, store,
    or transmit any user-generated content or personal information to our servers or any third
    parties.

Data Security:

We take data security seriously and have implemented appropriate technical and organizational
measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. Your
user-generated content remains stored on your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services:

Onotto does not integrate with any third-party services, and no information is shared with third
parties.

Children's Privacy:

Onotto is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect
personal information from children. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has
provided us with information without their consent, they should contact us at vmdevelops24@gmail.com, and we will delete such information.

Changes to Privacy Policy:

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the
application or on our website. By continuing to use Onotto after those changes become
effective, you agree to be bound by the revised Privacy Policy.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at vmdevelops24@gmail.com. By using the Onotto application, you agree to the terms and conditions outlined in this Privacy Policy. Please review this policy carefully, and if you do not agree with our practices, we recommend that you discontinue the use of Onotto.
